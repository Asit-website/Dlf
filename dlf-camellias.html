SUPER LUXURY RESIDENCES BY DLF CAMELLIAS

The Camellias architecture is aesthetically pleasing from the very first glimpse. Sixteen towers ranging from eighteen to thirty-eight stories create a distinctive profile against the setting sun, with the crowning tower that soars a brilliant one hundred fifty six meters into the air.

The Camellias is the latest residential offering in DLF’s acclaimed super-luxury portfolio. It follows in the footsteps of the legendary residences- The Aralias and The Magnolias. Over the years, these super-luxury residences have set pioneering benchmarks in offering unparalleled amenities, gorgeous views and truly personalized service.

The Camellias assimilates stunning architecture, breathtaking landscape design and the learning from DLF’s best developments across India to deliver a living experience far superior to anything experienced to date.

The Grand Masters

Hafeez Contractor

Renowned architect Hafeez Contractor has been associated with DLF since the 1980s. Of all the DLF projects he has been a part of, he acknowledges The Camellias as the one that makes him the proudest. Hafeez asserts that any project they undertake is “more than just a building." Therefore, he appreciates the incredible efforts made by DLF to transform The Camellias into something much grander, starting with the environment.

Shawn Sullivan

The Rockwell Group has designed inspiring hospitality spaces all around the world. Based in New York, these architects and designers have showcased their talent in the much-appreciated Nobu Restaurants, 15 Hudson Yards, The Casino at Marina Bay Sands and the Penthouse at the Greenwich Hotel.

Gerdo Aquino

There are some iconic projects across the world that companies would love to have in their portfolio. The Burj Khalifa in Dubai, Samsung American Headquarters campus, the Waldorf Astoria Beverley Hills Hotel, Shenzhen Bay and Stanford University campus are such; and one landscape architecture planning and urban design firm is associated with them all. SWA is based in California and has offices across the world.

Jay Wright

The Wright Fit is a global brand specialising in fitness centre design and management. They have worked on some prestigious properties worldwide, including 15 Central Park West and 220 Central Park South in New York, One Bennet Park in Chicago, 10000 Santa Monica Boulevard in Los Angeles, Lumina in San Francisco and the Palazzo del Sol in Miami.

Arnold Chan

Lighting can set the mood for life. Anyone who has come across the lighting design of Edition hotels, Public Hotel, the Phillippe Stark hotels or the Mandarin Oriental hotels will vouch for it. What they have in common is the design prowess of Isometrix Lighting + Design – experts in lifestyle lighting design. Arnold Chan, their principal designer points out how ‘lighting’ plays a pivotal part in making the occupant feel relaxed and comfortable within the space, at different times of day or night.

Ingo Schweder

Goco Hospitality Group is the world’s leading hospitality management and consultancy company with offices in Shanghai, Bangkok, Los Angeles and Berlin. Experts in feasibility, concept development, pre-opening services and on-going management, they have operated and set up around thirty-five prestigious projects around the globe, including Bulgari Hotels in Beijing, The Address Hotel and Palazzo Versace in Dubai, Capella Hotels, Four Seasons Hotel in Bangkok, The Ritz Carlton in Melbourne, the Spa at the Steigenberger Hotels Frankfurt and the Waldorf Astoria in the Maldives.
